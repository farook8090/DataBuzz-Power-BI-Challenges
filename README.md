# DataBuzz-Power-BI-Challenges
DataBuzz Power BI Challenges is a collection of interactive Power BI challenge projects focused on real-world business scenarios, data visualization, dashboard design, DAX calculations, and analytical storytelling. This repository showcases hands-on solutions, insights, and best practices for building impactful Power BI reports across various domains such as sales, marketing, finance, HR, and operations. Ideal for learners, analysts, and data enthusiasts looking to improve their Power BI and business intelligence skills.


# May Challenege - Accounting_firm_Audit_Compliance_Data

🚀 Successfully Completed the DataBuzz Power BI Challenge – Audit & Compliance Analytics!

Excited to share that I’ve completed the May 2026 DataBuzz Power BI Challenge focused on Accounting Firm Audit & Compliance Analytics. This challenge provided a hands-on experience in solving real-world business problems using Power BI, DAX, and audit analytics.

Throughout this challenge, I worked on:

📊 Audit Overview & Risk Analytics
Analyzed audit findings across departments, audit types, and financial years while tracking risk severity levels.

📑 Compliance & Regulatory Filing Tracker
Monitored filing compliance, late submissions, penalties, and regulatory reporting trends.

🛡 SOX / ICFR & Internal Control Analytics
Evaluated internal control effectiveness, process exceptions, deficiency severity, and remediation tracking.

🚨 Fraud Detection & Benford’s Law Analysis
Identified suspicious journal entries, anomaly patterns, and potential fraud indicators using analytical techniques.

📈 Audit Engagement Intelligence
Tracked audit efficiency, auditor utilization, budget vs actual hours, and engagement performance metrics.

This challenge helped me strengthen my skills in:
✅ Power BI Dashboard Development
✅ DAX & Data Modeling
✅ Audit & Compliance Analytics
✅ Business Storytelling with Data
✅ Risk & Fraud Analytics

A big thanks to Chiranjeevi K Power BI Expert and the DataBuzz team for creating such an insightful and industry-oriented learning experience. 🙌
Looking forward to participating in more real-world analytics challenges and continuing my learning journey in data analytics and business intelligence.
